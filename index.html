Dr Manjunath.M.K
Excellent doctor

Dr Manjunath MK is a Laryngologist based in Bangalore. After completing his Master of surgery (MS) in ENT from Rajiv Gandhi University of health sciences, Bangalore, he started pursuing his dream of becoming voice and airway surgeon. In this pursuit, he got trained himself under the internationally renowned laryngologist Dr. Paul Castellanos at the University of Alabama at Birmingham. He also underwent brief period s of training at Deenanath Mangeshkar hospital, Pune and Emory University, Atlanta, USA. He resisted the desire of staying back in the US and leading a comfortable life and came back to India so that he can utilize his skills to help the needy in his own country. He expertise in the field of advanced laryngology including bronchoesophagology. This includes taking care of voice, airway or the breathing problems, swallowing and snoring/ sleep apnea problems.
